Carreteiro de Charque

"Carreteiro de Charque!" Rice as you've never seen...

This is a typical Brazilian recipe. Arising from the need for fast, tasty and energetic food, ideal for the long journeys on horseback in the state of Rio Grande do Sul

The "charque" is salted and sun-dried meat with the aim of keeping it fit for consumption
for a longer period of time.

Ingridients

  • 1/5 kg of beef jerky
  • 3 cups of rice (tea)
  • 2 chopped onions
  • 1 minced garlic clove
  • Green spice to taste
  • Boiled eggs to taste
  • 6 cups (tea) of water
  • Pots
    • 1 bowl
    • 1 small pot
    • 1 medium pot

Directions

  1. Leave the beef jerky to soak in a bowl of water overnight in the fridge, changing the water whenever possible.
  2. Chop the beef jerky and boil for 5 minutes in a small pot.
  3. In a medium saucepan, sauté the onion and garlic until golden.
  4. Add the "charque" and sauté a little more
  5. Then add the rice and mix well.
  6. Add the water and cook until the rice is soft and moist.
  7. Serve with boiled eggs and green seasoning.
