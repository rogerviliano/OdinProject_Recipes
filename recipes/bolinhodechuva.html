Bolinho de Chuva

"Bolinho de chuva"! The raining days best friend!

Let's learn how to make a delicious Bolinho de chuva. A simple and taste recipe to do in raining days, perfect with a cup of coffee.

Says a legend that one afternoon in the 20th century, someone called them a rain dumpling.
Children needed to get out of the backyards and open environments to protect themselves from the rain and it was
the dumplings that put them inside the house and kept them properly occupied. Since then, the name has been linked to its recipe.

Let's make some!

Ingridients

  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup (tea) of sugar
  • 1/2 cup (tea) milk
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon of soy oil
  • 1 and a half cups of wheat flour
  • powdered cinnamon and sugar for sprinkling

Directions

  1. In a bowl, place the egg, sugar, milk and salt.
  2. Mix and stir well, add the baking powder and soy oil
  3. Stir a little more and add the wheat flour little by little
  4. Fry in very hot oil, dripping the dough with the help of a spoon
  5. After they are fried, just pass the dumplings in the mixture of sugar and cinnamon powder

Enjoy your coffee time watching the rain fall!
