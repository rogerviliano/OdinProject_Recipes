Chimarrão

"Chimarrão"! The best way to start the day!

Let's learn how to make a Chimarrão: a traditional hot tea (Mate) from South America.

It's very simple, in fact, to make a good "Chimarrão". But to do so, you're going to need some things.

Let's make some!

Ingredients

  • A recipient for the tea: a gourd. ( In brazil it's call "Cuia")
  • 2/3 recipient of "Erva Mate"
  • Hot Water ( approximately 70*C, or 158 *F)

Directions

  1. Fill two thirds of a gourd (the bowl where the mate is taken) with mate.
  2. Then, make a mound on one side of the gourd and pour warm or hot water up to the mouth.
  3. Wait 5 minutes before drinking to allow time for the herb to swell.
  4. The first sip should be spit out because it always comes with a little mate powder.
  5. If you want to repeat, you don't need to add more mate, just fill the gourd with water again.

Enjoy your drink with beloved ones!
